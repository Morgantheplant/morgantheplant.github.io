ABOUT: The SKU EXPLODER took me about 5 months to build, test, and debug. I decided to build it after spending full days retyping data from our buying logs into SWELL's Inventory Management program (Ecometry). The SKU EXPLODER can build 100 SKUs with fewer errors in about the same time it take takes to build 1 SKU manually -- about 5 minutes. This saves a tremendous amount of time and money considering the thousands of SKUs that are built every season. Today nearly all of SWELL's products use SKUs built by the SKU EXPLODER.

FORMAT: The green [DATA] tab is where the user inputs/pastes the SKU data (specifically in the light grey cells above). The orange [EXPLODE] tab is where the data is converted and where the majority of the functions live. The other tabs store tables that help check for errors and "explode" the scales (add COLOR + SIZE). In order to embed the SKU EXPLODER on this page, I stripped out the macros and reduced the input area to 3 rows from 100.

TRY IT: I filled out the top row with info (BILLABONG HELLO WORLD SS TEE BLUE), but you can enter your own data over it and in the other input fields as well. Scroll right to see if your data has errors and click the [EXPLODE] tab to check out the full functionality of the SKU EXPLODER. Below each input field is additional info describing it's function. Give your data the Size Table "morgan" and click the [EXPLODE] tab to see my hidden easter egg!

The SKU EXPLODER uses a thousands of nested functions to convert the data and then uses a single VBA button to delete excess lines depending on scale length. If your browser window is larger than 900px, than you can see some of the actual conversion code used as the background image for this page!
